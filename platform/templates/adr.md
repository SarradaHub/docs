# Architecture Decision Record Template

- **Status**: Proposed | Approved | Superseded | Rejected
- **Date**: YYYY-MM-DD
- **Deciders**: Names / Roles
- **Context**: 
  - What problem are we solving?
  - What constraints influence the decision?
- **Decision**:
  - Summary of the chosen option.
  - Alternatives considered and why they were rejected.
- **Consequences**:
  - Positive outcomes and benefits.
  - Risks, trade-offs, and mitigation plan.
- **Implementation Plan**:
  - Milestones / owners / due dates.
  - Testing & rollout considerations.
- **References**:
  - Related ADRs, issues, external documentation.
